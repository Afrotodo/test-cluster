1. Definitions

Personal Data means data about a living individual who can be identified from those data (or from those and other information either in our possession or likely to come into our possession).

Usage Data is data collected automatically either generated by the use of the Platform or from the Platform infrastructure itself (for example, the duration of a page visit).

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device (computer or mobile device).

Data Controller means the natural or legal person who (either alone or jointly or in common with other persons) determines the purposes for which and the manner in which any personal information are, or are to be, processed. For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, we are a Data Controller of your Personal Data.

Data Processor (or Service Provider) means any natural or legal person who processes the data on behalf of the Data Controller. We may use the services of various Service Providers in order to process your data more effectively.

Data Subject (or User) is any living individual who is using our Platform and is the subject of Personal Data.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to machine learning models, large language models (LLMs), natural language processing systems, and other automated technologies used to process, analyze, generate, or enhance content and search results on our Platform.

Automated Decision-Making refers to decisions made by AI or algorithmic systems without direct human involvement, including but not limited to search result ranking, content categorization, and business listing display order.

2. Types of Data Collected

We collect information about you when you use the Platform, including, but not limited to, information you provide when you register an account, access our content, update your profile, participate in a promotion, make a purchase, or contact our customer support.

Personal Data We May Collect

  • Geo-locational Information: Approximate location (city/region level) when enabled

3. Search Data & Artificial Intelligence

Important: AfroTodo is an AI-powered search engine, and search functionality is central to our Platform. This section explains how we handle your search data and how artificial intelligence is used across our services.

Search Data We Collect

When you use our search functionality, we collect:

  • Your search queries and keywords
  • Search timestamp and frequency
  • Whether you clicked on a search result
  • Session information linked to your search
  • Search refinements and filters used

How We Use Search Data

We use search data to:

  • Provide relevant search results
  • Improve search accuracy and relevance over time
  • Understand what our community is looking for
  • Generate aggregate insights about community interests
  • Develop new features and content categories
  • Train and improve our internal AI and machine learning systems

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

AfroTodo uses artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies to:

  • Process and understand search queries using natural language processing
  • Match you with relevant businesses, content, and resources
  • Categorize and organize content
  • Improve search results over time
  • Generate or enhance content displayed on the Platform
  • Rank and order business listings and search results

AI Training & Your Data

Your search queries and interactions with the Platform may be used to train and improve our internal AI systems that power search and content features. Specifically:

  • We may use aggregated and de-identified search data to improve our AI algorithms
  • We do not sell your personal search data to third parties for AI training purposes
  • We do not provide your personal information to third-party AI providers for the purpose of training their general-purpose AI models
  • When we share data with third-party AI service providers, it is solely for the purpose of providing services on our Platform, subject to contractual data protection obligations

Opt-Out: You may request that your data not be used for AI training purposes by contacting us at admin@afrotodo.com with the subject line "AI Training Opt-Out." Please note that opting out may limit certain personalization features on the Platform.

Third-Party AI Service Providers

We may use third-party artificial intelligence services, including large language models (LLMs) and other AI technologies, to power certain features of our Platform. When we use these services:

  • Your search queries and related data may be transmitted to third-party AI providers for processing
  • We require our AI service providers to maintain appropriate data protection and security measures
  • We contractually prohibit third-party AI providers from using your data for purposes beyond providing services to AfroTodo
  • Third-party AI providers may have their own data retention policies, which we review to ensure alignment with our privacy standards

We currently use or may use AI services from providers including, but not limited to, cloud-based AI and machine learning platforms. As AI technology evolves, we may engage additional providers. We will update this section as our third-party AI provider relationships change materially.

AI-Generated Content

Some content displayed on the Platform may be generated or enhanced by artificial intelligence. You should be aware that:

  • AI-generated content may not always be accurate, complete, or up to date
  • AI-generated content may reflect biases present in training data
  • AI-generated content is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute professional advice of any kind
  • You should independently verify any important information before relying on it

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and track information and to improve and analyze our Platform.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose
Session Cookies Operate our Platform and maintain your session while you browse
Preference Cookies Remember your preferences and settings
Security Cookies Security purposes and fraud prevention
Analytics Cookies Understand how visitors interact with our Platform

Other Tracking Technologies

Pixels: Also known as web beacons, these are code embedded in our Platform that sends information about your use to a server. Pixels are used in combination with cookies to track activity.

Local Storage: We may use browser local storage to maintain session information and improve your experience.

Your Choices

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Platform.

7. Retention of Data

We will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your Personal Data to the extent necessary to:

  • Comply with our legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our legal agreements and policies

Usage Data is generally retained for a shorter period of time, except when this data is used to strengthen the security or improve the functionality of our Platform, or we are legally obligated to retain it longer.

Search data and AI interaction data may be retained in aggregated or de-identified form for longer periods to improve our AI systems and search algorithms. Once data has been aggregated or de-identified, it is no longer considered Personal Data under this policy.

If you are located outside the United States and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the data, including Personal Data, to the United States and process it there.

When your data is processed by third-party AI service providers, it may be transmitted to and processed on servers located in various jurisdictions. We take steps to ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your data receives an adequate level of protection.

Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer.

12. Your Rights Regarding AI & Automated Processing

We believe in transparency about how AI and automated systems affect your experience on our Platform. You have the following rights:

Right to Know

You have the right to know when AI or automated systems are being used to process your data or make decisions that affect you, including how search results are ranked and how business listings are displayed.

Right to Opt Out of AI Training

You may request that your personal data not be used to train or improve our AI systems. To exercise this right, contact us at admin@afrotodo.com with the subject line "AI Training Opt-Out." Please note that previously aggregated or de-identified data cannot be removed from existing models.

Right to Human Review

If you believe an automated decision (such as business listing ranking, content moderation, or account action) has negatively affected you, you may request a human review of that decision by contacting us at admin@afrotodo.com.

Right to Explanation

You may request a general explanation of how our AI systems work and what factors influence automated decisions that affect you.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@afrotodo.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Your Rights

California residents may have the right to:

To exercise these rights, please contact us at admin@afrotodo.com.

16. Additional Disclosures for European Data Subjects (GDPR)

If you are from the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for collecting and using personal information depends on the Personal Data we collect and the specific context in which we collect it.

We may process your Personal Data because:

  • We need to perform a contract with you
  • You have given us permission to do so
  • The processing is in our legitimate interests and not overridden by your rights
  • For payment processing purposes
  • To comply with the law

Your Data Protection Rights

If you are a resident of the EEA, you have certain data protection rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: You can request that we delete your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we restrict processing of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to our processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent at any time

Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)

Our Platform uses AI and automated systems to rank search results, categorize content, and display business listings. These automated processes may affect the visibility of content and listings on the Platform. Under GDPR Article 22, you have the right to:

  • Not be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing that produces legal effects or similarly significantly affects you
  • Request human intervention in automated decisions
  • Express your point of view and contest automated decisions
  • Receive meaningful information about the logic involved in automated decision-making

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at admin@afrotodo.com.

You also have the right to complain to a Data Protection Authority about our collection and use of your Personal Data.
