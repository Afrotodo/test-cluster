Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my business on AfroTodo?

You can submit your Black-owned business through our Add Business page. Once submitted, our team will review and approve your listing within 24-48 hours.

Is AfroTodo free to use?

Yes! AfroTodo is completely free for users searching for businesses and resources and for businesses.

How can I update my business information?

Log into your account and navigate to your business dashboard to update your listing information, hours, photos, and more.

I found incorrect information. How do I report it?

Please use the contact form above and select "Feedback & Suggestions" to report any inaccuracies. We'll investigate and correct them promptly.

Do you offer partnership opportunities?

Absolutely! We're always looking to partner with organizations that share our mission. Contact us to learn more.

How can I advertise on AfroTodo?

We offer various advertising options for businesses looking to reach our community. Contact us to learn more about our promotional packages.
