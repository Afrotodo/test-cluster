We're Building the Search Engine for the Black World

A permanent, searchable, global infrastructure that maps Black excellence, preserves Black knowledge, and routes Black dollars back to Black hands.

About AfroTodo

AfroTodo ("Afro Everything") is an AI-powered search engine built for and by the Black community. We're not just connecting you to information—we're building the digital infrastructure for Black economic power.

Every day, algorithms decide what gets seen and what gets buried. Black businesses appear on page 10. Black history gets "fact-checked" into oblivion. Our stories are told by everyone except us. Our data is extracted and sold, but never used to empower us.

We're done asking those systems to do better. We're building our own.

Our Mission: Put data back into the hands of Black communities—where it belongs.

What We're Building

  • Find Black-Owned Businesses — searchable by location, category, and service, anywhere in the world
  • Discover Black Knowledge — history, culture, health, and news curated for us, by us
  • Preserve What Others Erase — a permanent record of our contributions that no algorithm can bury
  • Power Black Business Growth — data, visibility, and direct access to markets that want to support them
  • Unify the Diaspora — one searchable network connecting Africa, the Caribbean, America, Europe, and beyond

Meet the Founder

I'm Frank King, founder of AfroTodo. My background spans Electronic Engineering, Computer Science, and Data Science. I've built automation systems for marketing firms and data infrastructure for government agencies.

But my true calling is creating technology that serves communities—not extracts from them.

AfroTodo exists because I got tired of watching our businesses struggle for visibility while platforms profit from our attention. I got tired of watching our history get rewritten by people who weren't there. I got tired of waiting for someone else to build what we need.

So I'm building it. And I'm inviting you to build it with me.
